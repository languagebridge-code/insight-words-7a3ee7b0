The website is a focused 6-page conversion funnel:
1. Homepage (/) — Hero, trust badges, who we serve, feature invitation CTA
2. Features (/features) — Three tools, how it works, technical specs, pricing section
3. About & Media (/about) — Founder story, awards (Accelerate), press coverage, mission, values, roadmap
4. FAQ & Compliance (/faq) — Tabbed: General FAQ (by role) + Privacy & Compliance (FERPA, COPPA, SB 29, Title VI)
5. Privacy Policy (/privacy)
6. Terms of Service (/terms)

Redirects in place: /compliance → /faq, /pricing → /features#pricing, /media → /about

Hidden/utility routes: /admin, /teacher (PWA), /dashboard, /teacher-dashboard, /teacher-auth, /teacher-signup, /reset-password

Navigation structure: Features (direct link), Resources dropdown (FAQ & Compliance, About & Media), Get Started CTA
